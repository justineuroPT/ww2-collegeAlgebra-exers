Solution 5.8.1.

Solution: We use the result of the preceding problem and obtain:
\begin{equation*} \begin{array}{rcl} (a+bi)^3 \amp =\amp (a+bi)^2 \times (a+bi) \\ \amp =\amp \left(\left(a^2-b^2\right) + 2abi\right)\times (a+bi) \\ \amp =\amp a\left(a^2-b^2\right) + 2a^2bi +b \left(a^2-b^2\right)i+2ab^2i^2 \\ \amp =\amp a^3-ab^2 -2ab^2 +\left(2a^2b +a^2b -b^3\right)i \\ \amp =\amp \left(a^3-3ab^2\right) +\left(3a^2b -b^3\right)i \\ \end{array} \end{equation*}
