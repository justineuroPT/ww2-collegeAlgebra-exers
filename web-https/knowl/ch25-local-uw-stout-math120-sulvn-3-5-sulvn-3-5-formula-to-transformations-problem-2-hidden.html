Part I.
To obtain the graph of
\(y = f(x+5) - 6\)
from the graph of
\(y = f(x)\)
one needs to do the following transformations:
  • vertical shift 5 units up
  • horizontal shift 6 units right
  • horizontal shift 5 units left
  • vertical shift 6 units down
  • horizontal shift 5 units right
  • horizontal shift 6 units left
  • vertical shift 6 units up
  • vertical shift 5 units down
Note: select all transformations that apply
