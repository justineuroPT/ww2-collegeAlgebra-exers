The perimeter and area of a rectangle are given by
\(Perimeter = 2L + 2W\) and \(Area = LW\)
The perimeter and area of a square are given by
\(Perimeter = 4S\) and \(Area = S^2\)
The circumference and area of a circle are given by
\(Circumference = 2 \pi\ R\) and \(Area = \pi\ R^2\)
To find the total bill for the carpet, use the smallest area between the rectangle and square and multiply that area by the cost per square unit of carpeting.
