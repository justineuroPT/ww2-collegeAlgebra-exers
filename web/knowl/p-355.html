The complex conjugate of \((1+i)\) is \((1-i)\text{.}\) In general to obtain the complex conjugate reverse the sign of the imaginary part. (Geometrically this corresponds to finding the "mirror image" point in the complex plane by reflecting through the \(x\)-axis. The complex conjugate of a complex number \(z\) is written with a bar over it: \(\overline{z}\) and read as "z bar".
